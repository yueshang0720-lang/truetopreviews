## Choosing the Right Foundation for Your Website

Your web host is the foundation your website is built on. A good host keeps your site fast, secure, and online. A bad host means slow load times, frequent downtime, and frustrated visitors. Whether you are launching a personal blog, a business website, or an online store, choosing the right host is one of the most important decisions you will make.

## Types of Web Hosting

### Shared Hosting

Your website shares server resources with other websites — like living in an apartment building. It is the most affordable option ($1-15/month) and perfectly adequate for most small to medium websites. The trade-off is that heavy traffic on neighboring sites can affect your performance.

### VPS Hosting

Virtual Private Server hosting gives you dedicated resources within a shared physical server — like owning a condo. It offers better performance and more control than shared hosting at a moderate price ($20-80/month). Ideal for growing sites that have outgrown shared hosting.

### Cloud Hosting

Your website runs across multiple servers, allowing resources to scale up or down based on demand. Excellent for sites with variable traffic. Pricing is typically usage-based ($10-100+/month).

### Dedicated Hosting

An entire physical server dedicated to your website — like owning a detached house. Maximum performance, control, and security. Suitable for large e-commerce sites and enterprise applications. Pricing starts at $80/month and can exceed $500/month.

## Key Factors to Consider

### Uptime Reliability

Uptime is the percentage of time your website is accessible. Look for hosts guaranteeing 99.9% uptime or higher. In our 90-day monitoring tests, SiteGround achieved an industry-leading 99.99%. Even small differences matter — 99.9% vs 99.99% means 8.8 hours vs 53 minutes of potential downtime per year.

### Page Load Speed

Google uses page speed as a ranking factor, and visitors abandon sites that take more than 3 seconds to load. Look for hosts with fast server hardware, SSD storage, and CDN support. In our tests, A2 Hosting's Turbo servers delivered the fastest shared hosting speeds at 0.8 seconds average.

### Customer Support

When your site goes down at 2 AM, you need help fast. The best hosts offer 24/7 support via multiple channels. SiteGround's support team is the gold standard — knowledgeable, fast, and genuinely helpful. Phone support is valuable for urgent issues.

### Pricing Transparency

Nearly all web hosts use introductory pricing that increases significantly at renewal. A $2.99/month introductory rate might renew at $14.99/month. Always check the renewal rate before signing up. The longest initial term (typically 3-4 years) locks in the best overall rate.

### Essential Features

- **Free SSL Certificate**: Encrypts data between your site and visitors. Non-negotiable in 2026.
- **Automatic Backups**: Protects against data loss. Daily backups with easy restore are ideal.
- **One-Click Installers**: Simplifies installing WordPress and other applications.
- **Email Hosting**: Professional email addresses at your domain.
- **CDN Integration**: Content Delivery Network for faster global page loads.
