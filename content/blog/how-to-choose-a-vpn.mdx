## Why You Need a VPN in 2026

A Virtual Private Network (VPN) is no longer just for tech enthusiasts and businesses. With increasing online surveillance, geo-restricted content, and cyber threats, a VPN has become an essential tool for anyone who values their online privacy and freedom.

Whether you're working remotely, traveling abroad, or simply want to keep your browsing activity private from your internet service provider, a good VPN encrypts your internet connection and routes it through a secure server in a location of your choice.

## Key Factors to Consider

### 1. Speed and Performance

Not all VPNs are created equal when it comes to speed. A VPN will always introduce some latency because your data has to travel through an extra server, but the best VPNs minimize this to the point where it's barely noticeable. Look for VPNs that support modern protocols like WireGuard or NordLynx.

### 2. Privacy and Security

The most important feature of any VPN is its ability to protect your privacy. Look for AES-256 encryption, a strict no-logs policy (ideally one that has been independently audited), and a kill switch that blocks all traffic if the VPN connection drops.

### 3. Streaming and Geo-Unblocking

If you want to access streaming services from other countries, make sure the VPN you choose reliably works with the services you use. Netflix, in particular, aggressively blocks VPNs, so you'll need a provider that actively maintains its streaming servers.

### 4. Price and Value

VPN pricing varies dramatically. While you can find free VPNs, we generally recommend against them due to privacy concerns and limitations. Premium VPNs range from $2.50 to $13 per month, with significant discounts for longer subscriptions.

## Our Recommendation

After testing over 30 VPN services, we recommend NordVPN as the best overall choice for most users. It offers an excellent balance of speed, security, and features at a competitive price. For budget-conscious users, Surfshark provides remarkable value with unlimited device connections.
